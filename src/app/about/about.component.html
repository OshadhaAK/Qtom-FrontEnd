Who we are, What we do

We are...

We offer a range of customized electronic solutions to a number of industries including Industrial manufacturing, Consumer products from "One Off" custom designs to large scale production items.

Electronic Solutions can work with you on a number of levels, depending on your requirements.

service can range from a support role for existing products that require updating to current standards, to complete Research and Development projects to develop the products of the future. We encourage Australian designs and work with many local manufacturers to try and keep products made locally.

We enjoy learning new technologies and continually grow and add to our skills and abilities. We are passionate about product design and taking on new challenges

  • PLC Based Systens
  • PCB Design and Manufacturing
  • Schematic Designing
  • Firmware Developing
  • Customized Electronic Solutions
